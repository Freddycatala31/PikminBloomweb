INFORMATION-PIKMIN BLOOM

What's Pikmin Bloom?

Pikmin are mysterious plantlike creatures that live around us. However, they cannot be seen with the naked eye. Pikmin Bloom enables everyone not only to see Pikmin, but also to interact with them in their every day life!

Walk with Pikmin!

When you grow and pluck a Pikmin, they will immediately join your squad and follow you wherever you go!

Feed Pikmin fruit nectar to make the flowers on their heads bloom, so you can collect petals from them. You can then use these petals to plant new flowers when you go on a walk, leaving colorful trails behind you wherever you go and making Big Flowers bloom!

Finish off every day with a review of your activities, including how many steps you took and where you went. Save pictures to turn your lifelog into a real scrapbook that you can always use to look back on your memories!

Play with others!

Party Walk

With the Party Walk feature, anybody can host their own walking event. Once you set your group and time period, all you have to do is share your link/QR code with others and enjoy the remote walk!

Weekly Challenges

Step and Flower Planting Weekly Challenges start every week on Monday. You can choose to join with 4 friends, or to join a completely new group of people to work together towards a shared goal!

Postcards

Pikmin often bring postcards back from Expeditions and Mushrooms. You can save these postcards to look back on all the places you've visited, or you can choose to send them to your friends as a casual form of communication!

Mushrooms

When you go on walks, you may encounter Mushrooms of all types and sizes along your way. During certain periods of time, you may even find Mushrooms in colors that you'd never seen before! Destroying Mushrooms gets you rewards such as nectar and, during certain events, in-game items. Join forces with other users and march forth to tackle them!
